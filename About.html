    Hi, I'm Ellie..

    I've been a photographer for over 10 years, and in that time I've learned that the most meaningful images aren't posed — they're the quiet glances, the real laughs, the moments you didn't even know were happening. The photos that come out slightly blurry or over saturated.. every photo tells a story.

    My passion for photography started as a teen, from using my dads old point & shoot cameras to getting my very first DSLR. From shooting landscapes in the highlands, to the ruggid coastlines in the islands - the beauty of scotland brought my photography passion to life!

    I started professional photography a few years ago, capturing newborn photos for family, to eventually photographing my first wedding. Being able to share these memories with my clients brings me so much joy, a part of their life documented and saved for them to keep forever!
